      Add card

      Pressing the "Add Card" button on either side will allow you to search your card and add it to that side.


      Remove

      Hit the "Remove" button next to any card to remove it from the trade.


      Clear

      The "Clear" button will remove all cards on one side of the trade.


      Save

      The "Save" button will save your trade to your device so you can pick it up later.
